I like the part of engineering where an idea stops being a sketch and starts becoming a physical thing you can test, break, and improve.

Sustainable Water Pump Prototype

Designed and tested a lab-scale water pump system using rapid-prototyped impellers, a driven wheel assembly, tubing, and an iterative test rig. The project moved from SolidWorks geometry to printed parts and wet testing, with flow behavior and manufacturability guiding each revision.

SolidWorks · Design iteration · 3D printing

Vacuum Holder & Nozzle Assembly

Designed and iterated a compact holder around an existing vacuum-nozzle geometry, then prepared versions for additive-manufacturing tests. The workflow moved from native SolidWorks geometry to print-ready 3MF files for physical fit checks.
