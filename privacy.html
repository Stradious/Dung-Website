Privacy policy

Privacy at dungtriho.com.

Last updated June 29, 2026

This site is a personal engineering portfolio for Dung Ho. It collects only the information you choose to send through contact links or the opt-in build-list form.

Information Collected

The build-list form may ask for your name, email address, area of interest, and an optional message. If you email directly, your email address and message are received by the mailbox you contacted.

How Information Is Used

  • To respond to messages and collaboration requests.
  • To send occasional opt-in engineering, CAD, robotics, or R&D project updates.
  • To understand what kinds of project updates people want to receive.

Consent And Removal

The build list requires an explicit opt-in checkbox. You can ask to be removed at any time by replying to an email or writing to dungho.dth@gmail.com.

Sharing

Email addresses are not sold, scraped, or shared for unrelated marketing. Information may be handled by standard email or form services if those services are connected later.

Advertising

No Google AdSense code is active on this site right now. If ads are added later, this policy should be updated to explain cookie use, ad personalization, and any choices visitors have.

Contact

Privacy questions, update requests, or removal requests can be sent to dungho.dth@gmail.com.
